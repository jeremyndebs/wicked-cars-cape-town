About us

Wicked Cars (Pty) Ltd is a reputable auto mechanical and fitment centre with more than 13 years distribution and service experience in the motor vehicle industry. Our single minded purposes are “affordability, mobility and comfort-ability.” We call this our essence! We are a business that engages with one of people’s most valuable assets; “their vehicles”. Ensuring that safety and quality are met with the best products sourced locally and internationally. We are proudly 100% black-woman-owned and proudly boast a Level 1 Contributor procurement recognition level.
